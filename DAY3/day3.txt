CSS-OVERFLOW
        This property controls what happens to content that is too big
to fit into an area.
        The over flow property only works for the block element with 
a specified height.

the overflow property
-visible
-hidden
-scroll
-auto

1) Visible:-
      It is not clipped and it renders outside the element box.

2) Hidden:-
    The overflow is Clipped, and the rest of the content ishidden.

3) Scroll:-
    The overflow is clipped & a scroll bar is added to scroll inside
the box.

note:- this will add a scroll bar both horizontally & vertically.
                      - overflow-x
	     - overflow-y
4) Auto
     The auto value is similar tp scroll, but it adds scrollbars only when necessary.
